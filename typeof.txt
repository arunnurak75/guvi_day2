typeof operator : The typeof operator is used to get the data type of its operand.
typeof(1)           //the typeof operater is "number"
typeof(1.1)         //the typeof operator is "number"
typeof('1.1')       //the typeof operator is "string"
typeof(true)        //the typeof operator is "boolean"
typeof(null)        //the typeof operator is "object"
typeof(undefined)   //the typeof operator is "undefined"
typeof([])          //the typeof operator is "object"
typeof({})          //the typeof operator is "object"
typeof(NaN)         //the typeof operator is "number"]
